Privacy policy

Retime converts times on your device. It does not collect your browsing activity or send page content to us.

Last updated July 19, 2026

What Retime processes

On sites where you enable it, Retime temporarily reads visible page text to find and convert times. It may also read text you select when you use the context menu. This processing happens on your device. The text is not saved or sent anywhere.

What gets saved

Your time zones, appearance, site access choices, and other preferences are saved with Chrome storage. If you have Chrome Sync enabled, Google may sync those settings between your signed-in browsers. We do not receive them.

What Retime sends

Nothing from the pages you visit is sent to us. Retime has no analytics, ads, tracking pixels, remote logging, or server used for time conversion.

Optional payments and email

If you choose to support Retime, payment happens on Stripe's website. Stripe handles the information you enter there, and Stripe's privacy policy applies. We do not receive your full card number.

The report-a-bug links open your email app. Nothing is sent until you choose to send it, and we only receive what you include in the message.

Your choices

You can change Retime's site access and saved preferences at any time. Removing the extension removes its access. Chrome manages synced settings through your Google account.
